Re-Use of Tires as Planting Sites

The project proposes repurposing discarded vehicle tires as sustainable planting sites for kale cultivation in secondary schools. This initiative aims to address environmental sustainability by recycling waste materials and enhancing learners' agricultural knowledge through hands-on involvement.
